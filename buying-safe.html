Buyer's Guide

Buying Safely on OjaHub

Your safety matters to us. Follow these tips to have a smooth and secure experience on OjaHub.

Verify the Vendor

Before making any purchase, check the vendor's profile for ratings, reviews, and verification badges. A verified vendor has been reviewed by OjaHub for authenticity.

Communicate Clearly on WhatsApp

Always discuss product details, pricing, delivery terms, and timelines directly with the vendor before committing. Keep records of your conversations for reference.

Ask for Photos & Videos

Request real images or videos of the product or service before paying. Avoid vendors who are unwilling to share proof of what they're offering.

Be Cautious with Payments

Avoid paying the full amount upfront for high-value items. If possible, arrange payment on delivery or split payments to minimize risk.

Meet in Safe Locations

For in-person transactions, choose a public and well-lit location. Bring someone along if you can, and avoid sharing your home address with strangers.

Leave Honest Reviews

After your transaction, leave a review to help other buyers make informed decisions. Your feedback keeps the OjaHub community safe and trustworthy.

Important Reminder

OjaHub is a directory platform that connects buyers with independent vendors. We do not process payments, handle deliveries, or guarantee transactions. Always exercise caution and transact responsibly.
