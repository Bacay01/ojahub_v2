What is OjaHub? ⌄
OjaHub is a vendor marketplace that connects buyers with trusted local vendors across categories like food, fashion, beauty, gadgets, and services. We make it easy to discover and contact vendors directly.
Is OjaHub free to use? ⌄
Yes! Browsing vendors, viewing profiles, and contacting them via WhatsApp is completely free for buyers.
How do I contact a vendor? ⌄
Simply find a vendor you're interested in, visit their profile, and click the 'Chat on WhatsApp' button. You'll be connected directly with the vendor.
Does OjaHub handle payments? ⌄
No. OjaHub is a directory and discovery platform. All transactions, payments, and deliveries happen directly between you and the vendor. We recommend discussing all terms before making payments.
How do I know if a vendor is trustworthy? ⌄
Look for verified badges, ratings, and reviews on vendor profiles. We also recommend reading our 'Buying Safely on OjaHub' guide for tips on safe transactions.
Can list my business on OjaHub? ⌄
Absolutely! Click 'List Your Business' to submit your business details. Our team will review your submission and get your profile live
What categories are available? ⌄
We currently support Food Vendors, Fashion & Clothing, Beauty & Cosmetics, Phone & Gadgets, Services & Repair, and Home & Living. More categories are being added regularly
How do i report a vendor? ⌄
If you've had a bad experience, please contact us via WhatsApp or email with the vendor's name and details. We take reports seriously and will investigate.
