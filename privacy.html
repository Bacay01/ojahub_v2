Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Here's how we handle your information on OjaHub.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect basic information such as your name, email address, phone number, and location when you interact with OjaHub — for example, when listing a business or submitting feedback. We also collect non-personal data like browser type and usage patterns to improve our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to operate and improve OjaHub, display vendor profiles, facilitate buyer-vendor communication, respond to inquiries, and send relevant updates. We do not sell your personal information to third partiesrs.

3. Vendor Information

Vendor profiles — including business name, category, services, location, and WhatsApp contact — are publicly visible on OjaHub. By listing your business, you consent to this information being displayed on the platform.

4. Cookies & Analytics

OjaHub may use cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors use the platform. This helps us improve user experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may be affected.

5. Third-Party Links

OjaHub may contain links to external websites or services, including WhatsApp. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party platforms.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To make a request, contact us through our Contact Us page.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of OjaHub after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us via WhatsApp or email through our Contact Us page.
