How OjaHub Works

A simple guide to finding and connecting with vendors

🔍 Step 1: Search or Browse

Use the search bar or explore categories to find vendors that match your needs.

👀 Step 2: View Vendor Details

Check vendor profiles, services, location, and available offerings before making a decision.

💬 Step 3: Contact Vendor

Chat directly with the vendor via WhatsApp to ask questions or place an order.

🤝 Step 4: Complete Your Transaction

Agree on terms with the vendor and complete your purchase safely.

⚠️ Important Note

OjaHub connects users with vendors but does not handle payments. Always verify vendors before making transactions.
