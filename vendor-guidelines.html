VENDOR STANDARDS

Vendor Guidelines

To keep OjaHub a safe and trusted marketplace, all vendors are expected to follow these guidelines.

✔ Do's

Provide accurate and honest business information
Respond to customer inquiries promptly via WhatsApp
Deliver products/services as described and agreed
Maintain respectful and professional communication
Update your profile when your services or location change
Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews

✖ Don'ts

Misrepresent your products, services, or pricing
Ignore or delay responding to customer messages
Use abusive or threatening language with customers
Request unnecessary personal information from buyers
Engage in fraudulent or deceptive practices
Spam buyers with unsolicited messages

Violation of Guidelines

Vendors who violate these guidelines may have their profiles suspended or permanently removed from OjaHub. We are committed to maintaining a safe and trustworthy community.
