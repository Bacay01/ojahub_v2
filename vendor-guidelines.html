Vendor Guidelines

Follow these rules to maintain quality and trust on OjaHub

✅ Provide Accurate Information

Ensure your business name, services, pricing, and location are correct and up to date.

⚡ Be Responsive

Respond to customer messages promptly. Quick replies improve trust and visibility.

📸 Use Clear Images

Upload high-quality images of your products or services to attract customers.

💬 Communicate Professionally

Treat all customers with respect and provide clear, honest responses.

🚫 No Fraudulent Activities

Fake listings, misleading information, or scams will lead to immediate removal.

⭐ Maintain Good Reputation

Deliver quality service consistently to build trust and grow your business.

⚠️ Account Suspension

Vendors who violate these guidelines may be restricted or removed from OjaHub.
